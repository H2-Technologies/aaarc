Welcome to the Ashland Area Amateur Radio Club

Repeaters

The Ashland Area Amateur Radio Club operates three repeaters. There are two 2-meter repeaters and one 70-centimeter repeater. The 2-meter repeaters are located in Widowville, just south of Ashland, and at the WZLP Radio Tower in Loudonville. The 70-centimeter repeater is located 0.7 miles North of I-71 at mile marker 187. The repeaters are open to all licensed amateur radio operators. The club also operates a weekly net on the 2-meter repeater located in Widowville. The net is held every Thursday at 6:00 PM. All licensed amateur radio operators are welcome to check in. The net is a directed net and is used to pass traffic and to provide a forum for amateur radio operators to discuss topics of interest. The net is also used to provide information about upcoming club events and activities.
